Now that you know a little bit about my career choices, I can tell you more about
my life! I was born in Cape Cod, Massachusetts in the summer of 1988. After growing

environment, I ended up genuinely enjoying my time there. While in Pennsylvania, I attended
Shippensburg University and graduated with a degree in Studio Arts/Illustration. After making some
not so wonderful career choices, I eventually moved to Portland, Oregon in early 2016.
